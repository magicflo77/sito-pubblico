Centrosped Srl - Spedizioni Internazionali, with over 30 years experience gained in the international forwarding, has developed a firm and reliable competence in every import-export issues.

The air and ocean services are based on the main airports (Malpensa, Linate, Orio al Serio, Fiumicino) and on Italian and North European ports, where we co-operate with experienced partners.

Apart from the organization of air, ocean, trucking transports and temporary storage, the company offers a wide range of related services: customs issues, documentary credits and payments consultancy, drawing up of Intrastat declaration, Door-to-Door services via express couriers (DHL and UPS) with whom we have tailored contracts.

On international scale, we count on a close network of agents who allow us to organize the shipments up to the final destinations, mainly in the USA, South America and Far East Countries.

However, we have one flagship specialization: Cuba.
