Dangerous good

We are qualified for the transport of dangerous goods both by air and sea We provide the following services related to the shipping of dangerous goods:
  • - safety tested packaging solutions (for most of the goods)
  • - boarding documentation
  • - labelling of goods
  • - consultancy

Customer commitment

    The basic requirement for the customer is the identification of product through its technical data sheet.
