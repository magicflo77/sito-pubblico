Cuba Shipping

Since more than 15 years, we have been collaborating with the most important forwarding agencies in Havana, we have signed contracts with the main local companies involved in the import traffic from Italy and in the export to the European Union (EU) and South America.

We are registered member of the Italian Institute for Foreign Trade (I.C.E., Istituto nazionale per il Commercio Estero) as a specialized freight forwarder in Cuba where we regularly attend the Havana International Trade Fair, the most important international exhibition held in Cuba every year. We also coordinate the most freight shipments to be delivered to the exhibition.


Thanks to our competence and to the good name that Centrosped has developed in the island of Cuba, since 2007 we have been officially entrusted with the organization of all donations shipments from Italy to the the Cuban institution receiving the donations.
